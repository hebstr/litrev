# Protocol — Scoping Review

## Research Question

Quelle est l'épidémiologie des scapulalgies et pathologies de la coiffe des rotateurs chez l'adulte, en termes de prévalence, incidence, facteurs de risque et poids socio-économique ?

## Framework: PEO

| Component | Content |
|-----------|---------|
| **Population** | Adultes (≥ 18 ans) |
| **Exposure** | Scapulalgies, pathologies de la coiffe des rotateurs (tendinopathies, ruptures, syndrome d'impingement) |
| **Outcomes** | (1) Prévalence et incidence ; (2) Facteurs de risque (âge, sexe, activité professionnelle, comorbidités) ; (3) Reconnaissance en maladie professionnelle ; (4) Impact socio-économique (coûts directs/indirects, arrêts de travail) |

## Review Type

Scoping review (état des lieux pour l'introduction du protocole PRISE, CHU de Lille)

## Time Period

2010–2026

## Geographic Priority

Données françaises privilégiées dans la synthèse, données internationales incluses pour compléter.

## Databases

1. PubMed / MEDLINE
2. Scopus
3. Web of Science

## Search Concepts and Synonyms

| Concept | Terms |
|---------|-------|
| C1 — Pathologie | shoulder pain, scapulalgia, rotator cuff, rotator cuff tear, rotator cuff tendinopathy, supraspinatus, impingement syndrome, tendinopathy shoulder |
| C2 — Épidémiologie | epidemiology, prevalence, incidence, frequency, occurrence |
| C3 — Facteurs de risque | risk factor, occupational, work-related, age, sex, comorbidity, obesity, diabetes, smoking |
| C4 — Socio-économique | socioeconomic, cost, economic burden, sick leave, work disability, absenteeism, occupational disease, maladie professionnelle |

## Boolean Strategy

```
(C1) AND (C2 OR C3 OR C4)
```

C2, C3, C4 combined with OR — the question covers all three facets simultaneously.

## Inclusion Criteria

- Adult population (≥ 18 years)
- Shoulder pain or rotator cuff pathologies
- Reports at least one outcome: prevalence/incidence, risk factors, occupational disease recognition, socioeconomic impact
- Observational studies (cohort, cross-sectional, case-control), systematic reviews, meta-analyses, institutional reports
- Published 2010–2026
- Languages: English, French

## Exclusion Criteria

- Pediatric population (< 18 years)
- Acute trauma (fractures, dislocations)
- Exclusively interventional studies (therapeutic trials without epidemiological data)
- Case reports, letters to the editor, editorials without original data
