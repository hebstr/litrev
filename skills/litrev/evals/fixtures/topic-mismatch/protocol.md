# Protocol — Scoping Review: Rotator Cuff Tears and Comorbidities

## Research Question

What comorbidities are associated with rotator cuff tears and how do they influence surgical outcomes?

## Framework: PEO

- **Population**: Adults with rotator cuff tears (partial or full-thickness)
- **Exposure**: Comorbidities (diabetes, obesity, smoking, hyperlipidemia, thyroid disorders)
- **Outcome**: Prevalence of comorbidities, surgical outcomes (retear rate, functional scores), healing rates

## Review Type

Scoping review

## Time Period

2000–2025

## Databases

PubMed, Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex

## Search Concepts

1. Rotator cuff: rotator cuff tear, rotator cuff injury, supraspinatus tear, infraspinatus tear, subscapularis tear
2. Comorbidities: diabetes, obesity, BMI, smoking, hyperlipidemia, thyroid, metabolic syndrome
3. Outcomes: surgical outcome, retear, healing, functional score, Constant score

## Inclusion Criteria

- Clinical studies (RCT, cohort, case-control, cross-sectional)
- Adult patients with rotator cuff pathology
- Reports association between comorbidity and outcome
- English or French language

## Exclusion Criteria

- Animal or cadaver studies
- Case reports (n < 5)
- Pediatric population
- Adhesive capsulitis without rotator cuff involvement
