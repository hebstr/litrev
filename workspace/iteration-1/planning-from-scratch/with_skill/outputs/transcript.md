## Phase 1: Planning and Scoping

### 1. Research Question & Framework

The question — "efficacité des probiotiques dans la prévention des infections récurrentes à Clostridioides difficile" — is about an **intervention** (probiotics) and its **treatment effect** (preventing recurrence). The appropriate framework is **PICO**.

**Research Question**: In patients who have had a previous *Clostridioides difficile* infection (CDI), are probiotics effective in preventing recurrent CDI compared to placebo or standard care alone?

**PICO Framework**:

| Component | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **P** (Population) | Adults with a history of at least one *Clostridioides difficile* infection |
| **I** (Intervention) | Probiotics (any strain, formulation, dose, duration) administered as adjunctive or standalone therapy |
| **C** (Comparison) | Placebo, no probiotic, or standard of care alone (e.g., antibiotic therapy without probiotic supplementation) |
| **O** (Outcome) | Recurrence of CDI (primary); time to recurrence, adverse events, mortality (secondary) |

### 2. Scope

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Review type** | Systematic review |
| **Time period** | 2000--2026 (March) |
| **Geographic scope** | No restriction |
| **Study designs** | Randomized controlled trials (RCTs), quasi-experimental studies, prospective cohort studies with a comparator arm |
| **Language** | English, French |

### 3. Search Strategy

**Core concepts (4)**:

| Concept | Search terms (synonyms / MeSH) |
|---------|-------------------------------|
| **Clostridioides difficile** | "Clostridioides difficile" OR "Clostridium difficile" OR "C. difficile" OR "CDI" OR "C. diff" OR "CDAD" |
| **Recurrence** | "recurrence" OR "recurrent" OR "relapse" OR "reinfection" OR "re-infection" OR "repeat episode" |
| **Probiotics** | "probiotics" OR "probiotic" OR "Lactobacillus" OR "Saccharomyces boulardii" OR "Bifidobacterium" OR "Lactiplantibacillus" OR "live biotherapeutic" OR "synbiotic" |
| **Prevention** | "prevention" OR "prophylaxis" OR "preventive" OR "protective" |

**Boolean structure**:

```
(Clostridioides difficile terms) AND (Recurrence terms) AND (Probiotics terms) AND (Prevention terms)
```

**Databases** (4, meeting >= 3 requirement for systematic reviews):

1. PubMed / MEDLINE
2. EMBASE
3. Cochrane CENTRAL (Cochrane Library)
4. ClinicalTrials.gov

### 4. Inclusion / Exclusion Criteria

**Inclusion**:
- Studies evaluating probiotics for prevention of recurrent CDI
- Population: adults (>= 18 years) with >= 1 prior CDI episode
- Comparator arm present (placebo, no probiotic, or standard care)
- Outcome reported: CDI recurrence rate
- Study designs: RCTs, quasi-experimental, prospective comparative cohorts
- Published 2000--2026
- English or French language
- Peer-reviewed publications and registered trial results

**Exclusion**:
- Studies focused solely on primary CDI prevention (no prior episode)
- Fecal microbiota transplantation (FMT) as the sole intervention
- Animal or in-vitro studies
- Case reports, case series without comparator, editorials, opinion pieces, conference abstracts without full data
- Pediatric-only populations (< 18 years)
- Studies where probiotic was a minor component of a complex bundled intervention and its effect cannot be isolated
